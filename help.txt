How to install:

Place contents of gsprinter-master.zip into your addons folder.

Then open your addentities.lua and place the following code into the bottom of the file;

AddEntity("Topaz Printer", {
        ent = "topaz_money_printer",
        model = "models/props_c17/consolebox01a.mdl",
        price = 1000,
        max = 2,
        cmd = "/buytopaz"
})
